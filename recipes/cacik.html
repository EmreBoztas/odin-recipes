​A Refreshing Summer Delight: Turkish Cacık Recipe Recipes

Description: gCacık (pronounced jah-juhk) is a quintessential Turkish yogurt and cucumber dish, celebrated for its cooling properties and digestive benefits. Perfect as a side dish for nearly any meal or enjoyed on its own as a light snack, its simple preparation and refreshing taste make it a beloved staple, especially during the warmer months.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups full-fat plain yogurt
  • 1 cup cold water
    • ​
  • 4 medium-sized cucumbers
  • ​2 cloves garlic
  • ​Fresh dill, to taste (optional)
  • ​Salt, to taste
  • ​Dried mint, for garnish
  • ​Olive oil, for drizzling

Steps

  1. Prepare the Cucumbers: Wash the cucumbers thoroughly and peel the skins. You can then either grate them or dice them into very small cubes, according to your preference.

  2. Prepare the Yogurt Base: In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the full-fat yogurt and cold water until you achieve a smooth, homogeneous consistency.

  3. Combine: Crush or finely grate the garlic cloves and add them to the yogurt mixture. Stir in the prepared cucumbers and season with salt.

  4. Garnish and Serve: Transfer the cacık to a serving bowl. Garnish the top with a sprinkle of dried mint, finely chopped fresh dill, and a drizzle of high-quality olive oil.

  5. Serving Suggestion: For an extra refreshing experience, you can add a few ice cubes to the bowl just before serving. Enjoy it as a cooling side dish with grilled meats, rice pilafs, or as a standalone snack.
